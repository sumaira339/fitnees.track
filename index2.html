Shipping & Delivery

Order Processing: All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Shipping Times:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days

Shipping Costs: Costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. We may offer free shipping promotions.

International Shipping: We ship to select countries. Please note, customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes.

Returns & Refunds Policy

Eligibility: You have 30 days from delivery to return an item. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received.

How to Return:

  1. Contact our support team to get a Return Authorization (RA) number.
  2. Pack the item securely and write the RA number on the package.
  3. Ship the package to the address provided by our support team.

Refunds: Once we receive and inspect the item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges: For exchanges, please contact our customer support team for assistance.
